Three Cheese Brocoli Manicotti

Descriptions

Ingredients:

  • 2 large heads brocoli
  • 1 (8 ounce) package manicotti shells
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 cup ricotti cheese
  • 4 ounces grated provolone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups grated parmigiano-reggiano cheese, divided, or more to taste

  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pinch freshly grated nutmeg
  • 3 1/2 cups prepared marinara sauce
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 6 ounces cooked, crumbled, hot italian sausage
  • 1 tablespoon freshly chopped italian parsley, or to taste

Steps:

  1. Bring a pot of generously salted water to a boil.
  2. Meanwhile, separate broccoli stems and florets. Cut florets in half and cut each stem into several pieces lengthwise.
  3. Place stems in the boiling water and cook for 3 to 4 minutes. Then add florets and cook until just barely tender, about 3 more minutes.
  4. Use a slotted spoon to transfer broccoli to a bowl of cold water, leaving the water boiling on the stove. Let broccoli sit until cool, about 2 minutes. Transfer to a colander and let sit until very well drained. Leave the bowl of cold water for the pasta.
  5. Meanwhile, add manicotti to the boiling water and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender but slightly undercooked, 6 to 7 minutes. Use a slotted spoon to gently transfer manicotti into the bowl of cold water.
  6. Chop cooled, drained broccoli into small pieces. Transfer 3 cups chopped broccoli to a mixing bowl and reserve any extra for another use.
